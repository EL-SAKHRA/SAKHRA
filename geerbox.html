Shaft Inspection and Repair

We provide comprehensive gearbox shaft repair services, starting with a thorough diagnosis of wear, damage, or warping. Upon disassembly, our experts conduct meticulous inspections, assessing straightness, keyways, splines, and bearing locations. Using advanced techniques such as shaft straightening, precision machining, and metal spray build-up, we restore worn areas with precision. If a shaft is beyond repair, our replacement solutions maintain gearbox standards. After repairs, we ensure proper reassembly, optimal lubrication, and rigorous testing for seamless operation.
