Elsakhra stands as a prominent manufacturer of heavy-duty equipment components, serving a wide range of industries. Our expertise spans various manufacturing capabilities, establishing us as a comprehensive solution provider across multiple sectors.

gearbox

Our company specializes in manufacturing high-quality gears, including spur gears, helical gears, bevel gears, and worm gears.

shaft || rolls

We provide a full spectrum of shaft manufacturing services, including dimensional inspection, non-destructive testing, and failure analysis. Our expertise extends to shaft design with strength calculations, metallurgical analysis, reverse engineering, turning, and machining. Additionally, we offer custom tooling, fixturing, and the production of shaft liners or bearing sleeves. Our services also encompass dimensional verification, quality control, protective cover installation, and project management.

buckets

We are a leading manufacturer of heavy equipment buckets, providing a diverse range of products tailored to the needs of the construction, mining, and heavy-duty industries.
